Preserving the sanctuary. Keeping community at the center.

A family-led preservation and adaptive-use vision in Pigtown — protecting the sanctuary as a gathering place for reflection, music, fellowship, teaching, and community care, while exploring careful family stewardship for the long haul.

The idea

Sanctuary first. Family stewardship. Community care.

Pigtown Sanctuary Project is a preservation/adaptive-use vision rooted in keeping the sanctuary as the heart of the building and building a practical long-term family and community use around it.

Principles

Preserve the sanctuary. Respect the building. Keep the project human.

This is a family-led preservation and adaptive-use vision. The goal is not to erase the building’s past, but to keep the sanctuary alive, honor the neighborhood, and invite practical help, stories, referrals, and community conversation as the idea develops.
