Preserving the sanctuary. Restoring the home.

A family-led preservation and adaptive-use vision in Pigtown — keeping the sanctuary intact, restoring the residence, and building a long-term home for music, art, worship, making, and community.

The idea

Honoring the past. Building for the future.

Pigtown Sanctuary is a vision to preserve a historic sanctuary and restore the attached residence — creating a vibrant, welcoming place where creativity, faith, and community can thrive together for generations.
